Chat GPT

The most used application of AI


Chat GPT is an advanced computer program that can have conversations with people. It uses Artificial Intelligence (AI) technology to understand and respond to human language. Chat GPT has been trained on a diverse range of topics and can assist users by providing information, answering questions, or engaging in interactive conversations. How is AI used in Chat GPT? AI is used to train Chat GPT by feeding it lots of text from books, websites, and other sources. The program learns patterns and meanings from this text to understand and respond to human language. Use of Chat GPT: Chat GPT can be used to have fun and engaging conversations with a computer program. It can help answer questions, provide information, and even assist with tasks like language translation.
